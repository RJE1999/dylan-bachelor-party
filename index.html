KNOW BEFORE YOU GO

🔥 Weather

  • July in Scottsdale = 100-110°F+
  • Dry heat, but still brutally hot
  • Outdoor stuff early morning only
  • Pools, AC venues, and nightlife are your friend

🧳 Pack This

  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+, you will burn)
  • Swim trunks
  • Sunglasses
  • Going-out clothes for nightlife
  • Golf clothes (if we golf)
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Hangover kit (Pedialyte, Advil)

⏰ Time Zone

  • Arizona = Mountain Standard Time
  • AZ does NOT observe daylight saving
  • In July: same as Pacific Time (PDT)
  • 2 hours behind Minnesota (CDT)
